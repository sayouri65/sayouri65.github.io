🔒 Privacy Policy – FlagBar

Last updated: August 3, 2025
Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your information and ensure its safety.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

The FlagBar extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It does not use trackers, cookies, analytics tools, or external scripts that might access your activity.

2. How Does FlagBar Work?

FlagBar operates entirely in your browser. It modifies visual elements on selected websites (e.g., YouTube's progress bar) using identity flag color schemes (e.g., transgender, lesbian, bisexual).

3. Website Access and Permissions

Host Permissions

FlagBar requires access to specific sites like https://www.youtube.com/* to apply visual changes. It does not read content or browsing history - only CSS is modified.

Storage Permission

Used solely to remember the selected pride flag for YouTube. Preferences are stored locally and never shared.

4. Security

No remote code is used - all features, styles, and graphics are bundled within the extension. You maintain full control over what runs in your browser.

5. Contact

Questions? Reach out to the developer at czogista65@gmail.com.

6. Trust and Respect

FlagBar was created to affirm and celebrate diverse identities. We do not interfere with your data or browsing - your browser, your rules.
